What you need to know

The dopamine trap

Cheap dopamine hijacks your reward system with minimal effort. Over time, it raises your baseline — making genuine achievements feel hollow and boring tasks feel unbearable.

📱

Social scrolling

Endless feeds exploit novelty loops, firing small dopamine hits every few seconds without any real reward.

▶️

Binge watching

Autoplay removes the pause between episodes, locking your brain in passive, low-effort reward mode.

🍟

Junk food

Ultra-processed food is engineered to hit dopamine receptors faster and harder than whole foods ever could.

🔔

Notifications

Each ping creates a micro-anticipation loop — checking becomes a compulsive reflex within weeks.
